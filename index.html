Welcome to the world of NoizCode, the electronic music sensation hailing from Rhode Island. With a passion for electronic beats that traces back to the vibrant South Florida techno and breakbeat scene of the mid-'90s, NoizCode has spent over two decades crafting his unique sound and captivating audiences worldwide.

Pioneering Productions: NoizCode's musical journey has seen him release numerous tracks on platforms like Bandcamp and Soundcloud, each a testament to his mastery of electronic soundscapes. His extensive collection of DJ mixes, showcased on Mixcloud, reveals his deep understanding of the art of mixing and remixing.

Studio Savvy: As a former studio owner and contract worker for Project Nine Music Group, NoizCode has not only mixed and remixed but also co-produced various music styles. Through these experiences, he has forged his distinctive brand of beat-driven, heavy electronic music. Live and Thriving: NoizCode has been a regular fixture on the New England music scene, frequently gracing the stage alongside renowned acts such as Daedelus, Bill T Miller, Snowbeasts, Ana Dimco, Oxalis, Sunshine [at Night] and many more.

Techno Maestro: Over the past decade, NoizCode has honed his focus on techno music, infusing darker, heavier sounds into his high-energy, bass-driven style. The result is a hypnotic blend of pulsating, driving rhythms that transport listeners to a sonic dimension like no other.

Get ready to embark on a sonic journey with NoizCode. Whether you're a techno enthusiast or a casual music lover, his electrifying beats and mesmerizing performances are bound to leave you craving for more. Join us on this musical adventure and let the sounds of NoizCode transport you to the heart of electronic music.
